draw2d.OutputPort, draw2d.InputPort
class draw2d.Port
extends draw2d.Rectangle


A Port is the anchor for a draw2d.Connection. A draw2d.Connection must have a start and a end Port.

Version: 0.9.17

Author: Andreas Herz
