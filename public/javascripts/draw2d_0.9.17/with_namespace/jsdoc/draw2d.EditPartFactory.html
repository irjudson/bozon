draw2d.EditPartFactory

draw2d.EditPartFactory()
    A factory for creating new EditParts. EditPartViewers can be configured with an EditPartFactory. Whenever an EditPart in that viewer needs to create another EditPart, it can use the Viewer's factory. The factory is also used by the viewer whenever EditPartViewer.setContents(Object) is called.
    Version: 0.9.17

    Author: Andreas Herz
