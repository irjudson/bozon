void setSnapToGeometry( flag)
    Used to perform snapping to existing elements. Snapping is based on the existing children of a container. When snapping a rectangle, the edges of the rectangle will snap to edges of other rectangles generated from the children of the given container/canvas. Similarly, the centers and middles of rectangles will snap to each other.
